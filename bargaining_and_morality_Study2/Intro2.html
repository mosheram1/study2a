Welcome to Stage 2.

In this task, you will follow these steps in each round:

  1. Mentally choose one letter from the set A–F (do not click anything yet).
  2. You will then see a table where a random number (1–6) is assigned to each letter.
  3. Find the number under the letter you mentally chose and report it.
  4. Your partner will perform the same task independently.

Important: You and your partner will earn money only if both of you report the same number.

Payment: The amount paid is based on the number you both reported — each of you will receive half of that number, in USD.

This task will repeat for more than one round. Only your outcome from the final round will determine your bonus for this stage.
