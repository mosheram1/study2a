Welcome to the Study

Thank you for participating in this research study. The study will take about 15 minutes. Your responses are completely anonymous.

You will receive a base payment of $2.50. Additional earnings depend on your decisions and those of your partner(s).

The study has three parts:

  1. Stage 1 – Trust Game: You will interact with another participant and make decisions that affect both of your earnings.
  2. Stage 2 – Reporting Task: You and your partner will complete a task where matching decisions can lead to a bonus.
  3. Final – Questionnaire: You will answer a few short (non-identifying) questions.

Important Notes:

  • Your earnings in Stage 1 are determined by the interaction between two roles: Sender and Receiver.
  • In Stage 2, the task is repeated for multiple rounds, but only the result from the final round determines your bonus for that stage.
